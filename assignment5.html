KKR & KSR Institute Of Technology & Sciences is situated in Guntur in Andhra Pradesh state of India. Established in 2005, it is accredited from AICTE and it is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada. KITS GUNTUR, Guntur offers 10 courses across 2 streams namely Engineering, Management and across 3 degrees like B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA. KITS GUNTUR campus is spread over 15 Acres.
